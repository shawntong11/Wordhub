## What is WordCards?

WordCards is a flashcard-style vocabulary learning app that runs entirely in your browser. It comes with a curated, hand-built library of **thousands of English words** organized across 19 themed categories — from everyday conversation to academic, technical, political, and professional vocabulary.

Whether you're a student preparing for exams, a professional looking to sharpen your English, or simply someone who loves learning new words, WordCards gives you everything you need in one place.

---

## Features

**📖 Curated Word Library**
Thousands of words organized into 19 categories and over 200 subcategories — Campus, Work, Technology, Politics, Geography, Science, Arts, Sports, Food, and more. Every word has been selected and categorized by hand.

**🖼️ Image + IPA Cards**
Each word is paired with a contextual image and its IPA phonetic transcription, helping you connect meaning, sound, and memory at a glance.

**🎨 Color-coded Morphemes**
Key words highlight roots, prefixes, and suffixes in different colors — making it easier to recognize patterns and remember spelling across related words.

**🔊 Text-to-Speech**
Native American English pronunciation, with adjustable repeat count and interval. Hear each word exactly as it should sound.

**▶️ Auto-play Mode**
Sit back and let the cards play through automatically. Pause and resume any time.

**⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts**
- `←` / `→` — Previous / Next
- `Space` — Pause / Resume
- `S` — Speak current word

**📊 Progress Bar**
Click or drag to jump to any word instantly — works on both desktop and mobile.
